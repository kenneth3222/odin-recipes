What you will need

  • 1lb lean ground beef
  • 1 can heinz tomatoe sauce
  • 32oz riccotta cheese
  • 1 pack of lasagna pasta
  • parsely

instructions

  1. cook ground beef until cooked completely
  2. add tomatoe sauce and allow mixture to cook for 2 mins salt and pepper to taste
  3. cook pasta to aldente follow box instruction
  4. arrange baking ban of your choosing in the following order untill the pan is full or your run out of ingredients pasta
  5. bake in oven for an aditional 5 mins let sit for 20 mins
